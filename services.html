Our Services

Every reset starts with one small step.

Whether it's one room or the entire home, our goal is to leave you with a space that feels lighter, calmer, and easier to live in.

🏡 Fresh Start Reset

Our signature service. Perfect when life became overwhelming and your home needs a compassionate reset. We'll work room by room, restoring comfort without judgment.

🍽 Kitchen Rescue

Dishes. Counters. Garbage. Floors. The refrigerator. We'll help make your kitchen usable again so cooking one meal feels possible.

🛏 Bedroom Reset

Laundry mountains. Floors you haven't seen in weeks. Fresh bedding. Organized clothing. A bedroom should feel like rest—not stress.

🧺 Laundry Mountain

Sorting. Washing. Drying. Folding. Putting clothes away. Sometimes finishing the laundry is the biggest victory of the week.

🛁 Bathroom Refresh

A clean bathroom can make every morning feel just a little easier. We'll restore one of the most important rooms in your home.

🌿 Maintenance Visits

After a Gentle Reset, many clients choose occasional visits to help prevent overwhelm from returning. We're here for the long term if you need us.

What We Don't Do

Gentle Reset is not a biohazard, forensic, mould remediation, pest removal, or hazardous waste service. If your home requires specialized environmental or biohazard cleaning, we'll gladly recommend professionals who can safely help.

No shame. No lectures. Just help.

You don't have to explain how it happened.

You only have to decide that today is the day you'd like things to begin feeling a little lighter.
