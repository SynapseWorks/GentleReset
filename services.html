Our services

Every reset starts with one small step.

Whether it is one room or the entire home, our goal is to leave you with a space that feels lighter, calmer, and easier to live in.

Fresh Start Reset

Our signature service. Perfect when life became overwhelming and your home needs a compassionate reset. We work room by room to restore comfort without judgment.

Kitchen Rescue

Dishes, counters, garbage, floors, and fridge support. We help make your kitchen usable again so preparing one meal feels possible.

Bedroom Reset

Laundry piles, fresh bedding, floor clearing, surface cleaning, clothing organization, and a calmer place to rest.

Laundry Mountain

Sorting, washing, drying, folding, and putting clothes away. Sometimes finishing the laundry is the biggest victory of the week.

Bathroom Refresh

Sink, toilet, shower, floors, garbage, products, and basic organization. A clean bathroom can make every morning feel a little easier.

Maintenance Visits

After a Gentle Reset, some clients choose occasional follow-up visits to help prevent overwhelm from building again.

What Gentle Reset helps with

Depression and burnout cleaning, ADHD overwhelm, grief and life transitions, clutter reduction, laundry, dishes, surface cleaning, garbage removal, and practical home organization.

What we do not handle

Gentle Reset is not a biohazard, forensic, mold remediation, pest removal, or hazardous waste service. If your home requires specialized environmental or biohazard cleaning, we recommend working with professionals who are trained and insured for that work.

No shame. No lectures. Just help.

You do not have to explain how it happened.

You only have to decide that today is the day you would like things to begin feeling a little lighter.
